About Us

Printer World was created in 2024 by its founder Brady Kline, with the goal of giving customers a fast and cheap way to create 3D prints of their choosing.


Since our creation, we have dedicated ourselves to providing the best 3D printing service at the best cost for our customers.


We offer a wide range of 3D models that you can purchase and print on demand, as well as allow our users to upload their own 3D models for us to print here at Printer World!
